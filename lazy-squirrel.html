A zone is considered continuous if each nut(nut’s room) has at least one common edge with another nut of the same type.
Two nuts can be swapped or a whole row/column can be reversed. If there is no wall in the direction a nut is moved, then it will just exchange positions with the nut from the next room. In case there is a wall, or partial blocker(vegetation textured room), in the direction a nut is moved, nut’s positions will be reversed for the whole row/column. A row/column that contains partial blocker(s) will allow only partial nuts reversal: nuts from the opposite rooms of partial blockers won’t be moved.
A row/column that contains total blocker (stone textured rooms) will not allow row/column nuts reversal at all." - question: What are golden acorns and how to earn them? answer: "Golden acorns are Lazy Squirrel’s currency.
Get them by solving levels or requesting gift golden acorns in the main screen.
Use them to buy lives or bombs." - question: What is a bomb and where to use it? answer: "A booster that can be used to destroy a wall during gameplay: drag a bomb from the button located at the top of the game screen, align the cursor’s pointer with the wall to destroy(bomb cursor should become opaque) and drop the bomb." - question: A few words about lives? answer: "There can be a maximum of five lives.
A lost level causes the loss of a life, but after every twenty minutes the number of lives is increased by one. To get a life faster, use golden acorns, share Lazy Squirrel Facebook page on your wall or get a gift life (in main menu screen)." - question: How can I share my highscores? answer: "Use your G+ account to share a highscore or to see the leaderboard for the selected season(see buttons located in the bottom-right side of the levels panel in main menu screen).
Subsequent Google Play Services update and game restart might be needed." news_tag: lazy-squirrel ---

Lazy Squirrel

About the game

Lazy Squirrel invites you to test your nuts collecting skills. In an environment where the Squirrel is too Lazy to gather dried fruits, you step in and make sure it stays full. Deal with nuts’ rows reversal, nuts’ swap and blockers to form continuous zones for each type of nut thus reaching your goal.
